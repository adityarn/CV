\section*{Philosophy}
\noindent
The Southern Ocean is an important driver of the global climate. The oceanic thermal forcing on the ice sheets and their response to this forcing are a big unknown in the climate forecasts. As a scientist, I would like to do my part to inform the public and policy makers about the threats faced by vulnerable communities and ecosystems that are threatened by the impacts of rapid climate change. \\
